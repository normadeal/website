About Us

Learn More About Us

Perniagaan Normahas is an integrated pharmaceutical distribution company located in Malaysia's capital city Kuala Lumpur,which is driven by passion for enhancing quality of life through sustained and affordable healthcare. With a core team having a combined experience of 30+ years, we bring complete solutions in the major therapeutic areas, including oncology, cardiology, pain and analgesics, and many more. We are constantly expanding our offereings to meet the evolving needs of patients and healthcare providers, and we pride ourselves on staying at the forefront of the pharmaceutical industry.

About Alchemy Pharma

Alchemy Pharma involves in supply of niche product segments. As a special case, we have started our business with ophthalmology products. Humblely we have started our operation with zero customer and zero sales, after 2 years of inception we have implanted more than 2,000 monofocal lenses and supplying our ophthalmology consumables to almost all chain and private standalone Eye Care Centres and corporate hospitals like KPJ and IHH hospitals like Pantai Medical Centre, Bangsar.

Our Organisation

Mr Shahul Hamid

CEO

A first generation entrepreneur, Shahul Hamid stint in the Pharmaceutical industry goes back almost 2 decades. Starting in the 2000’s as an Analytical Research & Development pharmacist, he has scaled multiple heights to bring Normahas to its position today. Fascinated by the road less traveled, he has taken the company’s products to different corners of the globe, catering predominantly to the bottom of the pyramid with a combination of good quality and affordable products. He continues actively heading the organization in its multi-pronged growth approach for the years ahead.

Mr K. Balaji

CMO

Balaji started his career as Medical Sales Pharmacist in the year 2003. During his tenure with Sun Pharma India he was top achiever. His meticulous & passion towards Marketing & Scientific data generation drives him into Product Management & Marketing & he sticks with it for nearly 2 decades. He’s a part of Normahas management team from its inception. He’s envisioned with Normahas’s vision of catering predominantly to the bottom of the pyramid with a combination of good quality and affordable products.
